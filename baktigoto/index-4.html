About Deloitte Kenya

A little brief ABOUT the Company

The Kenyan firm is the longest established professional services firm in Kenya. Its history begins in 1907 with the formation of Ramsay & Gill. In 1915, this firm became Gill & Johnson and for several decades served many successful clients under this name before the eventual transition to Deloitte.
Today, with 24 Partners and 470 employees in offices in Nairobi and Mombasa, Deloitte is among the largest, most reputable firms in Kenya.
Deloitte Kenya offers services in Audit and Assurance, Consulting, Financial Advisory, Risk Advisory and Tax.

MY LEARNINGS

My Reflection

Successes and Failures

Successes: I was able to practice my skills on research, organization and presentation. I also made strong connections through networking with other colleagues

Failures: It was quite a challenge to balance work and studies.

What I would do differently

Looking back to my experience, if I was to do it all over again, I would ensure I request for more work than I did. I would also communicate my career goals to my manager and request to have tasks that would directly or indirectly align with my long term career goals.

Advice to future students/interns

Open-mindedness is an important quality anywhere, and especially when approaching a new work environment. If I was rigid and did not adjust my internship goals to get the most out of my opportunity, I would not feel as accomplished as I do.
I have learnt that one can learn much more than they expect when they approach any challenge or opportunity with an open mind.
