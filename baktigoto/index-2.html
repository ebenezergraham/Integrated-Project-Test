About Cellulant

Cellulant is a leading FinTech company that builds and operates mobile networks across Africa. It provides a one-stop payments platform by serving as the middleman that links end consumers, banks, merchants and mobile network operators together. Some of the services Cellulant offers include mobile banking, web payments, merchant payments, digital content and agency banking services.
Cellulant was co-founded by Ken Njoroge and Bolaji Akinboro in 2004 and launched as one of the first mobile content providers in Nigeria and Kenya. It has with time, evolved to offer other mobile commerce and financial payments services, with the aim of being a successful mobile commerce company in Africa, by Africans, and for Africans. It currently serves over 600 merchants, 94 banks, 44 mobile network operators and over 200 million customers across the continent.
