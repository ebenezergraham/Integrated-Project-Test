About Huawei Technologies

Background

Huawei Technologies is a chinese multinational headquartered in Shenzhen, China. The company is active in the industry of Information and Communications Technology (ICT). The company provides software and service equipment for carrier telecommunication networks and corporate computer networks. Established in 1988, the group has become a dominant supplier in China, and has won the international market by adopting a very aggressive price range. The list of its major economic competitors includes Cisco Systems, Alcatel-Lucent, Ericsson, Nokia Siemens Networks, Nortel, NEC, ZTE.

Size

Huawei has been growing steadily since its creation. In 2016, it had:

  • 180,000 employees, around 76,000 of whom are engaged in research and development (R&D)
  • US$ 75.103 billion in revenues
  • 21 R&D institutes in countries including China, the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Pakistan, Finland, France, Belgium, Germany, Colombia, Sweden, Ireland, India, Russia, Israel, and Turkey

Clients,products & services

Huawei is one of the world's leading providers of new networks generation for telecom operators. It offers a wide range of products, tailor-made innovative services, solutions and long-term growth for its clients.
The Huawei product catalog includes:

  • Wireless products (UMTS, CDMA2000, GSM / GPRS / EDGE, LTE and WiMAX).
  • Network products (NGN, xDSL, optical networks and communication of data)
  • Value-added services (intelligent network, CDN / SAN and data without wire).
  • Mobile and fixed terminals

Huawei's products and services have been deployed in more than 170 countries and it currently serves 45 of the world's 50 largest telecoms operators. Some of its clients:

  • Maroc Telecom, Orange and INWI (Morocco)
  • Vodafone, at&t, Singtel (international)

Advice to Future Interns

  1. For students who want to code during their internship in a Morocco, choose a small company that specializes in software development. It's rare to find corporates who develop their products in the country as they usually just provides services or products.
  2. For those who were stuck with something they didn't want, ENJOY the experience regardless. Your work environment will impact your experience more than the work, so get to know the people and make them know YOU.
