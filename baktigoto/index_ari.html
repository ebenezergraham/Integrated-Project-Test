About Thomson Reuters

THOMSON REUTERS CORPORATION IS A CANADIAN MULTINATIONAL FIRM ALSO KNOWN AS THE ANSWER COMPANY.

Founded in Toronto, Ontario, Canada as a result of the Thomson Corporation's purchase of British-based Reuters Group in April 2008, the company has grown to win many awards and was ranked as Canada's "leading corporate brand" in the 2010 Interbrand Best Canadian Brands ranking. Thomson Reuters operates in more than 100 countries, and has more than 45,000 employees across the world working in partnership with their clients to achieve the best productivity using the intelligent software solutions.

Thomson Reuters provides professionals with the intelligence, technology and human expertise they need to find trusted answers. We enable professionals in the financial and risk, legal, tax and accounting, and media markets to make the decisions that matter most, all powered by the world's most trusted news organization. With over 2000 clients in the Financial and Risk sector alone, Thomson Reuters is a company that's helping clients make impacts in their respective fields.

PROJECTS WORKED ON

Client Specialization

As a Client Specialist, my job involved a large amount of interaction with the Clients as well as a lot of opportunities to teach Clients about the functionalities of Thomson Reuters Solutions. This brought me to in depth learning about Thomson Reuters EIKON, how it works and ways that the systems unique functionality could be harnessed to find trusted answers to nearly questions one may have. I learnt the importance of filters, grouping data into searchable blocks as well as the value of relationships between various types pf data.

Market Development & New Business

I worked with the Thomson Reuters Market development team on two projects to further engage our already existing Clients and also reach out to potential Clients. These projects involved simulations and experientals that tapped into the pyschology of human tendency for emulation as well as a need to understand and know why, what, where and how things work. In addition to this, I also did some work on New Business Research which entails looking at a list of companies in various industries and carrying out research on them to see how Thomson Reuters solutions might be valuable to them.

Lunch Order System Development

I worked on a fully functional system to collect lunch orders from colleaugues at the office using tools by google such as google sheets and google forms as well as simplified the expense tracking system used within the office through a daily updated microsoft excel sheet with formulas to sum and do conversions. I was very involved in communications with our lunch vendors and managed relationships with some of them in collaboration with colleagues

Innovation Labs

The Thomson Reuters Innovation Labs consists of a global team of data scientists, research scientists, full stack developers, and designers; specializing in data science & analytics, data visualization, artificial intelligence and blockchain looking to unlock potential value of data, and apply cutting edge technologies to emerging challenges faced by professionals. While wroking at Thomson Reuters I worked on a research project that looked into how we could help start-ups and enterprises as a company by innovating our already existing flagship solutions.

Advice To Future Students

  • Keep an open mind, there's a lot to be learned if you're looking well enough
  • Ask questions, you can never ask too many questions
  • Pay attention to work that others are doing, it could be of help to you
  • Try to build a relationship with co-workers
  • Whatever you do, do to the best of your ability
  • Learning non-major related things is still learning
  • Take care of your self as you work
  • Be patient with yourself
